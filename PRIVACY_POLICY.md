# Privacy Policy for CF Stopwatch

**Last Updated: January 4, 2026**

Your privacy is critically important to us. **CF Stopwatch** is built with a "privacy-first" philosophy. This policy explains how we handle your information.

## 1. No Data Collection
CF Stopwatch does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal information. 
- We do not use cookies or tracking pixels.
- We do not have any external servers or databases.
- We do not collect device IDs, IP addresses, or location data.

## 2. Permissions Used
The app requires the following permissions for specific technical reasons:
- **Display over other apps (Overlay):** Required to show the floating stopwatch while you use other applications.
- **Foreground Service:** Required to keep the timer running accurately when you navigate away from the app.

## 3. Third-Party Access
Because we collect no data, we do not share any data with third parties. There are no advertisements or third-party analytics SDKs (like Firebase or Facebook) integrated into the app.

## 4. Open Source Transparency
CF Stopwatch is an open-source project. You are welcome to review our source code on GitHub to verify our privacy claims:
https://github.com/fishdan/stopwatch

## 5. Contact
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer via the GitHub repository issues page.
