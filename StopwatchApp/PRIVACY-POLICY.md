# Privacy Policy for Stopwatch App

Last updated: 2025-12-29

This Privacy Policy describes how Fishdan.com ("we," "us," or "our") handles your information when you use our Stopwatch mobile application.

## 1. Information We Collect
The Stopwatch app is designed to function without collecting any personally identifiable information (PII). 
- **Personal Data**: We do not collect names, email addresses, or phone numbers.
- **Usage Data**: We do not track your usage of the app.
- **Location Data**: We do not access your device's location.

## 2. Permissions
The app requires the following permissions to function correctly:
- **Foreground Service**: To keep the stopwatch running while the app is in the background.
- **System Alert Window (Overlay)**: To display the stopwatch over other apps.

## 3. Third-Party Services
We do not share any data with third-party services.

## 4. Security
We take reasonable measures to protect any data that may be stored locally on your device, though no sensitive data is collected by the app itself.

## 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
